My top 3 Movies of all Times

Megan

Robot movie, fun to watch. Teaches the importance of parents in our life alongwith the devastating effects of bringing robot to too much use in our life.

Laapata Ladies

Inspiring, nice and eye opening. Talks about importance of education and the side effects of patrirarchial society. Motivates young women especially from rural cities to achieve their academic and future desires.

Truth or Dare

Scary (for me atleast). The movie revolves around the game of truth and dare except for it is linked to paranomal activites and a haunted past.
